About IET BU

IET BU, or the Institute of Engineering & Technology at Bundelkhand University in Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh, is a public institution established in 1975. It's affiliated with Bundelkhand University and is approved by AICTE and MHRD, New Delhi, as well as by the UP Board of Technical Education and the State Government of UP. The institute offers a variety of engineering courses, including B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, and MBA/PGDM, with specializations like Electronics & Communication Engineering, Information Technology, and Mechanical Engineering.

Key features of IET BU:
Established: 1975.
Affiliation: Bundelkhand University.
Approvals: AICTE, MHRD, UP Board of Technical Education, State Government of UP.
Course Offerings: B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, MBA/PGDM.
Specializations: Electronics & Communication Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, etc.
Accreditation: Approved by AICTE.
Additional Information:
The institute also has a Department of Food Engineering and Technology.
The institute has a placement ratio of 70% with the highest package around 17 Lacs and the lowest around 4 Lacs, according to Shiksha.
Top recruiting companies include Larsen and Toubro and HLS Asia according to Shiksha.
The institute's website www.bujhansi.ac.in/en is also known as BU Jhansi..
